What is PUSO Spaze?

PUSO Spaze ("puso" means heart in Filipino) is an anonymous peer support community where you can share your struggles, receive encouragement, and find hope — without fear of judgment.

Built for Filipinos

Designed with the Filipino Gen Z community in mind — blending faith, culture, and mental health support in one compassionate platform.

AI-Powered Encouragement

Our "Hourly Hope" feature delivers AI-generated biblical encouragement in Taglish (Tagalog + English), crafted for Gen Z hearts that need a reminder they're not alone.

Truly Anonymous

Post with a randomly generated username or choose your own. Your real identity is never revealed. Share freely, heal openly.

Coach-Moderated Safety

Trained PUSO Coaches review flagged content to keep the community safe. Harmful posts are caught by AI moderation and human review.

React & Support

Show support through Pray and Care reactions. Leave encouraging comments with @mentions. Every interaction is a chance to lift someone up.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy and safety are our top priorities.

No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect your real name, email, phone number, or location. Your identity within PUSO Spaze is limited to your chosen or auto-generated username.

Device Binding

Your username is linked to your device via a secure, randomly generated device ID stored locally. This helps you keep your identity across sessions without creating an account.

What We Store

We store your posts, comments, and reactions on our secure servers. Posts go through AI moderation to ensure community safety before being published.

Data Deletion

You can clear your device binding at any time from your Profile. Coaches and admins can remove individual posts or comments. Contact us to request full data deletion.

AI Moderation

Posts are reviewed by OpenAI's content moderation API to detect harmful content. This is done to protect the community — no personal data is shared beyond the post text.

Push Notifications

Push notifications are opt-in and only used for reactions, comments, and mentions on your posts. You can disable them at any time.

No Tracking or Ads

PUSO Spaze does not use analytics trackers, advertising networks, or sell any user data to third parties. Ever.
