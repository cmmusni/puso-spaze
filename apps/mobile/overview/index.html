A safe space to share.
A community that cares.

Not everything needs to be posted publicly.
Not every struggle needs a name attached to it.

PUSO Spaze was created for the quiet battles —
the unspoken prayers, the late-night worries,
the thoughts you're not ready to say out loud.

Here, you can share anonymously.
Here, you can be heard without being exposed.
Here, someone will pause… and pray for you.

💭 Why We Built This

Social media connects people — but it doesn't always make people feel safe.

Many young people today carry anxiety, pressure, doubt, and spiritual questions in silence. We believe no one should have to walk through that alone.

PUSO Spaze was built to create a digital sanctuary — a modern community space where vulnerability is protected and compassion is practiced.

This isn't about likes.
It's about lifting one another up.

Protected by AI, Powered by People

We use AI technology to:

  • Filter harmful or abusive content
  • Maintain a respectful environment
  • Encourage positive, supportive responses

But the heart of PUSO Spaze is not AI.

It's people.

Real people choosing to pray.
Real people choosing to encourage.
Real people choosing kindness.

AI protects the space — Community fills it.

✨ Features

😶‍🌫️
Anonymous Posting

Share your thoughts freely with generated anonymous usernames. Your identity stays private.

🕊️
Hourly Hope Companion

AI-powered biblical encouragement that posts every hour and also adds an instant contextual encouragement comment after you share.

🛡️
AI Moderation

Smart moderation helps filter harmful or abusive content and keeps community conversations safe and respectful.

💬
Comments & Reactions

Support others with thoughtful comments and emoji reactions (❤️ 🙏 💪 🌟 😊).

🔔
Push Notifications

Get notified when someone reacts to your post, comments, or when new encouragement arrives.

👨‍⚕️
Coach Dashboard

Special interface for mental health coaches to send invites and oversee community wellness.

🔮 Future Plans

💬
Spaze Buddy Chat

Private AI chat companion for confidential support, biblical advice, and active listening whenever you need someone to talk to.

🔍
Search & Filter

Find posts by keywords, tags, or date range to discover relevant content.

👥
Support Groups

Create or join topic-specific support communities for shared experiences.

📚
Resource Library

Curated mental health resources, biblical articles, and coping strategies.
