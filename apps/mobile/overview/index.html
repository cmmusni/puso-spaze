How to Install the Web App

Add PUSO Spaze to your home screen in seconds — no app store required.

1
Open Safari Navigate to puso-spaze.org in Safari. Safari is required on iPhone and iPad.
2
Tap the Share button Tap the Share icon (□↑) at the bottom of the screen (or top on iPad).
3
Add to Home Screen Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4
Confirm & open Tap "Add" in the top-right corner. PUSO Spaze appears on your home screen like a native app!
Safari is required on iOS/iPadOS. Chrome and other browsers on iPhone or iPad do not support home screen installation.
1
Open Chrome Navigate to puso-spaze.org in Google Chrome on your Android device.
2
Tap the menu Tap the three-dot menu icon (⋮) in the top-right corner of Chrome.
3
Add to Home Screen Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app" from the menu.
4
Confirm & open Tap "Add" or "Install". PUSO Spaze will appear on your home screen!
Google Chrome is recommended for the best PWA experience on Android. Some Android browsers may show an install banner automatically.
1
Open Chrome or Edge Navigate to puso-spaze.org in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.
2
Click the install icon Look for a computer/install icon (⊕) on the right side of the address bar and click it.
3
Install the app Click "Install" in the popup. PUSO Spaze opens as its own standalone window!
No install icon? In Chrome, go to Menu (⋮) → "Save and share" → "Install page as app". In Edge, go to Menu (…) → "Apps" → "Install this site as an app".
