# Project Brief — PUSO Spaze

## Overview
PUSO Spaze is a compassionate mental health support platform providing a safe, anonymous space for individuals to share their struggles, receive encouragement, and find hope through community support and AI-powered biblical encouragement.

## Core Requirements
- **Anonymous posting** — Users can share struggles without revealing identity
- **Community engagement** — Reactions (Pray, Care, Support), comments, mentions
- **AI moderation** — OpenAI content filtering for safety
- **Biblical encouragement** — Personalized AI-generated daily reflections in Taglish for Gen Z
- **Coach moderation** — Human review dashboard for flagged content
- **Private journaling** — Personal reflection entries with mood tracking
- **1:1 support chat** — Direct messaging between users and coaches
- **Cross-platform** — Web + iOS + Android from a single Expo codebase

## Target Audience
- Filipino Gen Z individuals seeking mental health support
- Community coaches providing guidance and moderation
- Admins managing platform safety and invite access

## Design Identity
"The Sacred Journal" — a warm, safe, journal-like aesthetic with deep berry primary color (#7C003A), tonal surface layering (Material Design 3 style), Plus Jakarta Sans + Be Vietnam Pro typography, and ambient shadows (never harsh borders).

## Success Metrics
- Safe, moderated community space
- Active engagement (reactions, comments, journal entries)
- Coach responsiveness to flagged content
- Cross-platform feature parity
